Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Sandwich made with melted cheese

Introduced early in childhood in the US, this sandwich is everything from a snack to a meal to a comfort food.


Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of choice, white is often used
  • Several slices of cheese of your choice
  • 1, 2 tablespoons of butter or mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Heat 10 inch skillet
  2. Spread butter on one side of one slice of bread
  3. Spread butter on one side of the other slice of bread
  4. Put slices of bread butter side down into pan
  5. Place cheese on one slice of bread
  6. Place other slice of bread butter side up on top of the cheese
  7. Heat until cheese is melted. Flipping several times may be necessary
  8. Enjoy!
