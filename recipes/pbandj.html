Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Sandwich made with Peanut Butter and fruit jelly

Introduced early in childhood in the US, this sandwich is everything from a snack to a meal to a comfort food.


Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of choice, white is often used
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1, 2 tablespoons of fruit jelly, I prefer grape

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread
  3. Put slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other
  4. Enjoy!
